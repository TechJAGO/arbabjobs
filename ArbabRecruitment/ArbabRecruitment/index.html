About Arbab Jobs

Your trusted partner in international recruitment

Who We Are

Arbab Jobs is a premier recruitment agency specializing in connecting skilled Indian professionals with leading employers across the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries including UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, and Oman.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the talent gap between India and the GCC region by providing transparent, efficient, and personalized recruitment services that benefit both job seekers and employers.

Our Services

Comprehensive recruitment solutions for every need

Executive Search

Specialized recruitment for C-suite and senior management positions across various industries in the GCC region.

Corporate Staffing

End-to-end recruitment solutions for companies looking to hire professionals at all levels from India.

Visa & Documentation

Comprehensive assistance with visa processing, work permits, and all necessary documentation for international employment.

Candidate Support

Pre-departure orientation, cultural training, and ongoing support to ensure smooth transition to GCC countries.

Skill Assessment

Career Counseling
